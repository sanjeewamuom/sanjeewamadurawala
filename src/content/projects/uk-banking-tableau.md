Created a regional banking dashboard in Tableau using data from Scotland, Northern Ireland, England, and Wales. Includes customer age and balance distributions, gender breakdown, and job profiles—with slicers for age, balance, and gender plus cross-filtering for dynamic exploration of banking trends and demographics.
