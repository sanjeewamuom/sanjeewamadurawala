About Me

Hello, I'm Sanjeewa Madurawala, a Data Analytics Engineer and BI specialist based in Sri Lanka. I focus on enterprise Power BI, Microsoft Fabric, operational analytics, and AI-assisted development workflows that help teams make faster, better decisions.

I am currently a Power BI Engineer at Raketman (Vandermarliere Family of Cigars), where I own and deliver reporting across supply chain, logistics, inventory, production, and finance—from SAP-integrated operational dashboards to group-level visibility for leadership and shop-floor teams.

Before that, I spent over two years at Dialog Network Services in Analytics & Automation, building operational analytics for telecom infrastructure, disaster recovery, SLA management, and workforce operations.

My path started in electrical engineering (BSc from the University of Moratuwa) and plant operations across power generation and manufacturing, before I moved fully into data and BI. I completed an OTHM Level 7 Diploma in Data Science in 2024.

Explore my project portfolio for sample dashboards and analyses, or see my full resume for experience, skills, certifications, and education. I'm always open to new opportunities—feel free to reach out.
