## Context

As Power BI Engineer for a global manufacturing and supply chain organization, I serve as the primary data owner for enterprise operational reporting. Solutions span supply chain, logistics, inventory, production, finance, and group-level operations.

## Scope

Reporting domains include end-to-end operational visibility: warehouse performance, bill-of-materials analysis, inventory and consumption forecasting, procurement planning, and executive group dashboards. Stakeholders range from shop-floor supervisors to the Group CEO.

## Approach

1. **Requirements** — Lead workshops with business stakeholders; translate needs into technical specifications and scope documents.
2. **Design** — Proof-of-concepts and interactive mockups to validate data models and UX before build.
3. **Standards** — UI/UX design standards for consistency across enterprise reports.
4. **Build** — Semantic modeling, advanced DAX, report design, performance tuning, and deployment.
5. **Governance** — Workspace governance, refresh scheduling, security roles, RLS, and cross-environment deployment.
6. **Validation** — Data accuracy checks across frontend reports, backend sources, and query logic.

## Solution structure

| Layer | Focus |
|-------|--------|
| **Sources** | SAP operational data integrated into automated pipelines (replacing manual Excel workflows) |
| **Model** | Scalable star schemas, optimized refresh pipelines, advanced DAX for high-volume operational reporting |
| **Reports** | Domain-specific dashboards: Warehouse KPIs, Tobacco Budget, Cigar Composition, Tobacco Duration |
| **Features** | Calculation Groups, Field Parameters, RLS, Power BI Service workflows |
| **Delivery** | Managed workspaces, scheduled refresh, role-based access for managers through executive level |

## AI-assisted development

Agentic coding with Cursor and Claude Code accelerates report development, DAX authoring, debugging, version control, documentation, and data transformation automation—while maintaining governance and quality standards.

## Outcomes

Solutions are actively used for data-driven decision making across operations and leadership. SAP automation reduced reporting effort and turnaround time. Advanced Power BI patterns ensure consistency, security, and performance at enterprise scale.
