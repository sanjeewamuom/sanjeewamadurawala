## Context

In a regional operations analytics role for a national telecom infrastructure provider, I built operational intelligence systems supporting field teams, disaster recovery, and senior management planning.

## Scope

Analytics covered telecom infrastructure monitoring, disaster recovery coordination, SLA management, workforce operations, electricity budget optimization, and infrastructure reliability—including critical alarms and risk analytics.

## Approach

1. **Operational monitoring** — Dashboards for real-time infrastructure health, alarms, and escalation tracking.
2. **Disaster management** — Prioritization analytics for field response and resource utilization during large-scale failures.
3. **Risk & reliability** — Battery health, design backup monitoring, and critical alarms to reduce downtime and safety incidents.
4. **Workforce & SLA** — Task completion, escalation, and regional visibility for operations teams.
5. **Executive support** — Evidence and insights for senior management on resource allocation and infrastructure planning.

## Solution structure

| Domain | Analytics focus |
|--------|-----------------|
| **Infrastructure** | Site monitoring, alarms, battery/backup health, reliability KPIs |
| **Disaster recovery** | Prioritized response, resource optimization during outages |
| **Energy** | Electricity spend analytics for multi-billion LKR operational budget |
| **Workforce** | SLA tracking, task completion, escalation workflows |
| **Safety** | Critical alarms and risk analytics supporting incident prevention |

## Outcomes

Delivered 20+ solutions improving operational visibility across regional teams. Disaster management systems enabled faster, data-driven field response. Electricity analytics supported optimized use of a large annual operational budget. Infrastructure risk analytics contributed to achieving zero fire incidents and reduced critical downtime scenarios.
