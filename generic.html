About Me

Hello, I'm Sanjeewa Madurawala, a dedicated and versatile Data Analyst and BI Developer based in Sri Lanka. With a background in Electrical Engineering and a current role in data analytics, I have successfully transitioned into the field of Computer Science to pursue my passion for data.

I hold a BSc in Electrical Engineering from the University of Moratuwa and am currently pursuing a PG Diploma in Data Science. My career journey began in the Electrical Engineering sector, but due to the industrial situation in Sri Lanka, I pivoted to Computer Science, where I have found my true calling.

Currently, I work in Analytics & Automation at one of the leading Telco Providers in Sri Lanka. In this role, I specialize in creating and managing BI dashboards that are crucial for optimizing organizational performance and achieving key performance indicators.

I am proficient in Power BI, SQL, Python, R, Tableau, and other data visualization and analysis tools. My passion for continuous learning and technology has driven me to self-learn and excel in creating effective and efficient data solutions.

In addition to my technical skills, I enjoy tackling challenges and learning new technologies. I am particularly interested in machine learning, deep learning, and AI, and I am eager to apply these skills to real-world problems.

Feel free to explore my portfolio to see some of the projects I have worked on. I am always open to new opportunities and collaborations, so don't hesitate to reach out!
